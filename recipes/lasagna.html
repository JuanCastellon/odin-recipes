Lasagna Recipe

Description

This lasagna recipe features three kinds of cheese, sweet Italian sausage, and lean ground beef for a rich and creamy final dish. A very simple and easy to follow recipe for a quick meal for the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 gloves of garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans of canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • ½ cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons of freshly chopped parsely, divded
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 ½ teaspoons of dried basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon of Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon of fennel seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces of ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • ¾ pound cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Gather all your ingredients
  2. Cook the sausage, ground beef, minced onion, and the two cloves of garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons of parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon of salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 ½ hours, stirring occasionally.
  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse in cold water.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese with egg, the remaining 2 tablespoons of parsley, and ½ teaspoon of salt.
  6. Preheat the over to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  7. To assemble, spread 1 ½ cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lenghtwise over meat sauce, overlapping slightly. Spread with ½ ricotta cheese mixture. Top with ⅓ of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 ½ cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with ¼ cup Parmesan cheese.
  8. Repeat layer, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, ether spray foil with cooking spray or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.
  9. Bake in the preheated over for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  10. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
