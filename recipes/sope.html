Sope Recipe

Description

This simple sope recipe is quick and easy for some delicious appetizers suitable any occasion. Serve with any topping you like!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of masa harina (Maseca is fine)
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 2 cups of warm water, or as needed
  • 1 quart of oil for frying

Directions

  1. Whisk masa harina and salt together in a bowl. Stir in enough water until dough is smooth and holds together.
  2. Form the dough into twelve 2-inch balls. Place balls onto parchment paper and flatten to Â½ inch thick circles.
  3. Heat oil in a large deep fryer or large saucepan to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  4. Working in batches, fry a few sopes at a time until lightly browned, about 30 seconds per side. Transfer with tongs to a paper towel-lined plate and let cool just until they can be handled, 2 to 3 minutes. Pinch the edges of the sope togeher to form a little boat.
  5. Return sopes to the hot oil and fry until golden and cooked through, 1 to 2 more minutes. Drain on the paper towels.
  6. Add any topping you'd like! We recommend a combination of cooked ground beef, refried beans, guacamole, and cheese!
